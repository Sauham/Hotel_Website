THE OBEROI

A landmark of Mumbai since 1940's

The Oberoi Hotel Mumbai, located at Nariman Point, is an iconic landmark that stands as a testament to visionary hospitality and excellence.Built in 1973, the hotel initially operated as a joint venture with Sheraton under the name “Trident Hotel.” In 1979, Oberoi took full ownership and renamed it “The Oberoi Towers,” which was later shortened to its current name.
